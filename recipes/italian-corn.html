Description

Prep Time & Cook Time: 5 mins.

Therefore the total time to cook will be 10 mins. The recipe will serve 4 people.

Nutrional Facts about the dish 124g Calories 6g Fat 19g Carbs 3gProtein

Ingredients

  • 1.5 tablespoons butter
  • 2.5 cups whole kernel sweet corn
  • 1 tablespoon italian seasoning
  • 1 pinch salt and ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Heat a skillet over high heat. Heat butter in hot skillet until almost completely melted;stir in corn. Season corn with italian seasoning, salt, and black pepper. Cook seasoned corn until light golden brown, about 5 minutes.
